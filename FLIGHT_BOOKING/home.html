Popular Domestic Airlines

AIR INDIA

Air India is the flagship airline of India, owned by the Tata Group. Established in 1932, it is known for its extensive domestic and international network, offering premium services and a rich legacy in Indian aviation.

INDIGO

IndiGo is India’s largest airline by market share, known for its low-cost model, punctuality, and extensive domestic and international network. Founded in 2006, IndiGo operates a modern fleet of Airbus A320, A321, and ATR aircraft, focusing on affordable and hassle-free travel.

VISTARA

Vistara is a premium full-service airline in India, a joint venture between Tata Sons and Singapore Airlines. Launched in 2015, it is known for its luxury experience, superior hospitality, and comfortable seating across Economy, Premium Economy, and Business Class.

SPICE-JET

SpiceJet is one of India's leading low-cost carriers, known for its budget-friendly fares and extensive domestic network. Established in 2005, the airline operates a fleet of Boeing 737 and Bombardier Q400 aircraft.

GO-FIRST

Go First, previously known as GoAir, was a budget airline in India that focused on providing affordable travel options. It operated a fleet of Airbus A320 aircraft and connected various domestic and international destinations.

AKASA AIR

Akasa Air is India’s newest airline, launched in 2022, with a focus on affordability, sustainability, and customer-friendly service. Backed by billionaire Rakesh Jhunjhunwala, the airline operates a fleet of Boeing 737 MAX aircraft.
